Artificial Intelligence and Biotechnology

  • Amy Nick
  • Aug 03, 2020

Developments in artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) are playing an increasingly influential role in the biotech space, driving the formation of new partnerships between the tech and healthcare industries. While big pharma is increasingly seeking collaborations with AI startups, major tech players such as Google, IBM and Microsoft are taking steps into the biotech space. A 2019 survey of pharmaceutical and biotech professionals by ICON found that 80% of respondents were using, or planning to use, AI technologies. - Nick Amy

Artificial Intelligence; the Future of Education for Kids

  • ROBOGARDEN
  • Mar 02, 2020

Artificial intelligence is formulating a huge part of our everyday life, everything is becoming “smart”, starting from mobile phones and even ending with parking places. The 21st century proved that coding is the language of the future and kids are supposed to gain this skill early in their lives in order to fit in the future, and this is part of what artificial intelligence and robotics are, that’s why AI in education should definitely be the future of schools. In order to let kids understand why everything around them is turning to become smart, they should grasp the whole idea of how robots might be able to do everything in the future, they might even let people stay at home to get their jobs done, which is exactly the same reason why kids should have the skills that would make them competitive in the future.
